Notes

Feudal Landmark: Majority of the time you want to go Aachen chapel. Meinwerk is only viable if you plan to all-in with Burgrave Palace. Castle Landmark: If against boom civs build Regnitz, if all-in castle or against aggression/harass Burgrave is best choice. Castle: Tactical focus is to apply some pressure then push for Imperial. IF you built Burgrave, mass some infantry then force a fight and harass. Remember you do not need a monastery for your relics. Put them in your towers. Attempt to get at least 2 relics every game. IF you built Regnitz, harass with Knights. You start with 2-4 and if the enemy is spread and does not have a lot of defences (Spears) you can build up to 12 and cause havoc. If they already have a Spear mass don't make anymore Knights, keep them safe and try and apply pressure where you can. General military comp is Infantry/Cav. MMA/Horse or Knights for example.
